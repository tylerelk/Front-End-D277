Los Angeles - The City of Angels

At a glance...

  • Population: 3,898,747
  • Incorporated: April 4, 1850
  • Region: Southern California
  • Setting: Urban
  • Median household income: $49,497

Nicknames for Los Angeles include Entertainment Capital of the World, La-la-land, and Tinseltown
Los Angeles, often referred to by its initials L.A., is the most populous city in the U.S. state of California. With roughly 3.9 million residents within the city limits as of 2020, Los Angeles is the second-most populous city in the United States, behind only New York City; it is also the commercial, financial and cultural center of Southern California. Los Angeles has an ethnically and culturally diverse population, and is the principal city of a metropolitan area of 13.2 million people.
